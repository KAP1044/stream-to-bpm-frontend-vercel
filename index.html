This beta app helps you enrich your music playlist with BPM (Beats Per Minute) values using track metadata.

How to use

  1. Export your playlist from YouTube Music using a Chrome extension like YouTube Music Exporter.
  2. Ensure the exported file is a .csv with track names in a column.
  3. Go to the uploader below, upload your file, and get an enriched CSV with BPM info.
